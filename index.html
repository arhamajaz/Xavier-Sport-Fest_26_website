Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my college team?

Click on the "Register Now" button and complete the multi-step registration form. You'll need to provide college details, select sports, and submit required documents. You'll receive a confirmation email after successful registration.

What is the registration fee?

Registration fees vary by sport: â‚¹5,000 per team for team sports (Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Cricket, Kabaddi) and â‚¹1,000 per player for Table Tennis. Payment details will be provided after initial registration.

Are accommodations provided?

Yes, accommodation in university hostels is available for outstation teams at subsidized rates. You can indicate your accommodation requirements in the registration form. Limited spots available on first-come-first-served basis.

What documents are required for registration?

You need to submit: College identity proof/authorization letter, list of participating players with ID proofs, and recent passport-size photographs of all team members. All documents can be uploaded during the online registration process.

What safety measures are in place?

We have comprehensive safety protocols including: on-site medical team and ambulance, certified first-aid responders, sports injury specialists, insurance coverage for all participants, and 24/7 security personnel throughout the venue.

Can I participate in multiple sports?

Yes! Your college can register teams for multiple sports. However, ensure there are no schedule conflicts. Individual players can participate in more than one sport if schedules permit and they meet eligibility criteria for each sport.

Is the EDM night open to everyone?
